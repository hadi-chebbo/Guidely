## The Problem

Many students in Lebanon face real challenges when choosing a university major:

- They don't fully understand what each major involves
- There is no clear connection between majors and the **Lebanese job market**
- They lack insights about salaries, demand, and career opportunities locally
- Existing platforms mainly list universities without offering real guidance

This leads to poor decisions, dropped programs, and wasted years.

---

## The Solution

MajorPath is a complete guidance platform tailored for Lebanon. It helps students understand:

- What each major actually involves day-to-day
- Career opportunities both **locally and internationally**
- Market demand and trends specific to Lebanon
- Required skills for each path
- Honest pros, cons, and challenges

The goal is not just to inform — but to **guide real decisions**.

## Core Features

### Majors Explorer
- Browse majors available in Lebanese universities
- Categorized, searchable, and filterable

### Major Details Page
Each major includes a full breakdown:
- Overview & description
- Study duration
- Pros & Cons
- Job opportunities (local & international)
- Required skills
- Key challenges
- Market demand in Lebanon

### Comparison System
Compare multiple majors side by side based on:
- Duration
- Difficulty
- Salary expectations
- Market demand
- Skills required

### Universities Integration
- Lebanese universities offering each major
- Public / Private classification
- Admission requirements & additional details

### User Accounts
- Save favorite majors
- Track comparisons history
- Personalized experience

### Smart Recommendations *(Premium)*
- AI-based major suggestions based on user profile
- AI assistant for career & major questions
- Advanced analytics and deeper career insights
